Four Design Element in Web Design.

Design Elements

  1. Contrast
  2. Repetition
  3. Alignment
  4. Proximity

Contrast: Catch the interest of user

  • The conflict of warm and cold color is nice contrast combo.
  • The size of the words are contrast.
  • The fonts that was being used are also a contrast.


Repetition: Showing something that was already show again

  1. Shows you different videos in a same form.
  2. Have a clean design that made it easier dfor people to find what they like to see.
  3. logo is clear and doesn't standin the way of viewing.


Alignment: Element are organized in order

  1. All images in side are organized and users can understand what does it sell just by reading the front image.
  2. Cumbersome but easy to understand design.


Proximity: related elements should near each together

  1. All the element are next to each other in a small distance.
  2. A clean design that can navigate people when needed.
